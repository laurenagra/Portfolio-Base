Meet Lauren

Hi, my name is Lauren (but please call me Sunny~)! I've spent much of my life taking risks and accepting challenges in order to push myself. Between moving across the world alone, to starting my career from scratch, I have rarely ever backed down from a challenge set before me. The arts have always been an interest of mine, starting from young when I joined chorus. I haven't stopped singing since expanding my creative endeavors, and can proudly say that I've been singing for 20 years, and am able to use this skill when practicing foreign languages. My next big goal is to hone my web design skills and be able to create interactive webpages in languages like Spanish, Korean, German, and English alike!
